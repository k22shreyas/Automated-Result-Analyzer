A Novel approach to analyze results on vtu university portal

This is a site made for extracting results from the vtu result portal.
The csv files of student usn and subject codes are to be uploaded.
Click the execute button which initiates our automation code.
The respective usn's and subject codes are utilized in the extraction process.
Finally after the extraction processs is complete, download the final files provided by clicking download.
